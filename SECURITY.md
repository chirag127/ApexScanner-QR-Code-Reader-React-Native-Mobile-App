## Reporting a Vulnerability

Use this section to tell people how to report a security vulnerability.

Please report security vulnerabilities to us privately.
We will appreciate your efforts to responsibly disclose your findings, and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a security vulnerability, please send an email to [your-email@example.com](mailto:your-email@example.com) with the subject line "SECURITY: Vulnerability in ApexScanner".

We will respond to your report within 24 hours.
We will keep you informed of our progress and will let you know when we have a fix.

We will not take any legal action against you as long as you act in good faith and follow the responsible disclosure guidelines.
We will not disclose your personal information to third parties without your permission.

We are looking forward to working with you to make ApexScanner more secure.
